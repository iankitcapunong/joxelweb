# Revenue Cycle Management
Managing your revenue cycle requires a strategic process for healthcare organizations to improve their revenue projections while maximizing their bottom line. Joxel’s Revenue Cycle Management process is a 360 degree view of the organization’s business, focusing on your admission/registration, patient mix, payer mix, authorization, discharge, billing and claims, denials and AR management to ensure organizations are maximizing revenue. From a value-based care standpoint, Joxel provides analytical solutions to leverage your data in making effective decisions on managing risks and increasing revenue within your population.
