# About Us
Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed citizens can change the world; indeed it’s the only thing that ever has. – Margaret Mead


### Our Story
Our company was founded in 2006 by Sushil and Nan Pillai with the intent to create a business consulting and development firm that was industry-specific and focused on enhancing the client’s goals and increasing their business efficiency. Our logo was created to remind everyone our organizations goal is to excel at everything we do while adding value to our clients. We strive to create a large impact with our clients so they will want to come back to us, rather than being required.


### Our Focus
Healthcare and Pension. We focus on five core areas: healthcare consulting, configuration, support, custom support and outsourcing.

We leverage business and technical insights to ensure our clients are fully aware of the decisions they make while focusing on the long-term growth of their organizations. We work with our clients to understand the detailed operations of our clients so that we can make a sustainable impact on their organization. Our focus allows our team to become well-acquainted with business trends and technology innovations in the healthcare and pension industries which support our commitment to innovate, improve and deliver high quality results to our clients.


### Our Promise
We judge our success on every client engagement by adherence to our core values:

- Innovation – We continuously seek and implement our service delivery to reduce our clients cost and time of execution.
- Ownership – We carefully define the scope of our responsibilities and take personal ownership of our responsibilities. Failure to exceed client expectations is never acceptable. We never accept work we cannot deliver with excellence. We acknowledge and correct our mistakes.
- Integrity – We are driven by an ethical incentive to provide our clients with high moral solutions that improve their business and the communities they serve.
- Value Creation – We strive to build long lasting relationships with our clients, we are driven to improve our clients business performance and reduce their business risk.
