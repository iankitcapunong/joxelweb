# EHR Optimization

## Our experience drives your success.
High quality client care requires strong clinical processes, workflows and data. Incomplete documentation and under-performing EHRs lead to physician push back, quality concerns and impact to your revenue cycle.

Joxel will collaborate with you to redefine processes and enhance utilization of your EHR through:

- Clinical documentation analysis and process improvement
- Revenue Cycle assessment and process improvement
- Decrease AR days
- Claims, denials and collections management

Joxel delivers innovative and hands on health care consulting to improve clinical and revenue cycle workflows, ensure system adoption and provide your organization with the ability to use your EHR data for population health management, value based reimbursement and outcomes.
