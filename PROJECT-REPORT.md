# Website Revamp and Programmatic SEO Architecture (Ian)

A complete redesign and rebuild of thejoxelgroup.com to reflect The Joxel Group's
AI‑forward positioning and full service capabilities.

**Status: Delivered** — 107 pages, live‑deployable (Vercel‑ready), repo: `iankitcapunong/joxelweb`.

## Deliverables

**Modern website redesign and optimized user experience**
- Full visual rebuild with a premium, soft‑tone design system, refined typography and layered depth.
- Brand‑accurate palette derived from the logo (teal · navy · cyan) applied site‑wide.
- Smooth interactions: scroll reveals, animated stats, hover states, seamless client‑logo marquee.
- Fully responsive across desktop, tablet and mobile (validated for zero horizontal overflow).

**Clear positioning across all core service offerings**
- EHR Consulting, Revenue Cycle Management, and a new AI Strategy offering, each with dedicated pages.
- Original site content preserved verbatim; individual leader bios rebuilt as internal pages.

**Programmatic SEO sitemap covering major U.S. metropolitan markets**
- Programmatically generated `sitemap.xml` (107 URLs) + `robots.txt`.
- 24 major U.S. metros covered (New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Dallas, Houston, DC, Philadelphia,
  Atlanta, Miami, Phoenix, Boston, San Francisco, Detroit, Seattle, Minneapolis, Denver, Tampa,
  Charlotte, St. Louis, Milwaukee, Nashville, Columbus, Portland, Madison).

**Dedicated location pages for:**
- EHR Consulting — 24 metro pages
- Revenue Cycle Management — 24 metro pages
- AI Strategy — 24 metro pages
- (72 location pages total, plus a Locations hub and per‑service hubs.)

**Unique, locally relevant content for every page**
- Content composed from real geographic data (state, region, metro, neighboring cities) woven
  through multiple template variants — verified **0 duplicate‑content pages** across the site.
- Localized headings, intros, FAQs and internal links for every market.

**Structured schema markup**
- Per‑page JSON‑LD on all location pages: `Service` + `areaServed`, `FAQPage`, `BreadcrumbList`,
  and `Organization`; Organization schema on the homepage.

**Search‑optimized architecture designed to avoid duplicate content and AI‑generated content penalties**
- Self‑referential canonicals on every page; unique titles and meta descriptions (107/107).
- Real local data + varied templates instead of boilerplate; deep internal linking between
  services and nearby markets.
- Clean, accessible markup: one H1 per page, alt text on all images, labelled forms, reduced‑motion support.
