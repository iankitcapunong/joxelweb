Our Leaders

Nan Pillai

Chief Operating Officer

Nan has over 20 years of management consulting experience working in the financial services, healthcare, and high-tech software development industries. Over the years, she has worked as a project manager, subject matter expert for customer relationship management software, and technical consultant for various software development projects. Currently, Nan is responsible for the administrative operations for Joxel, including HR, Payroll, Benefits, and IT needs within the company. Prior to joining Joxel, Nan has worked at Cuna Mutual Group, Health Risk Management, and Vestica Healthcare.
