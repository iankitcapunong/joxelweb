The LSS Partnership Circle

Our relationship with Lutheran Social Services (LSS) began in 2018 when our Executive Leadership Team engaged in an assessment of their Information Technology processes and utilization of their EHR. Upon successful completion, our team continued this partnership with an IT Steering Committee implementation and an optimization project with the intent to increase utilization, delivery of care and ROI within their EHR. As a result of the constant collaboration and due to the tremendous commitment Lutheran Social Services has to improving our local communities, The Joxel Group, LLC was invited to join the LSS Partnership Circle in 2020.
